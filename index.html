Welcome to Luke Ken!

Luke Ken is a free and open-source game that also provides a collection of free assets and resources for use. These assets, initially created for DN3D Engine, they are available for use in various game projects beyond DN3D Engine.

Screenshots

Story

Luke Ken is a sci-fi game where you play as a futuristic Space Enforcer who works for the Galactic Task Force (G.T.F. for short). After your return to Earth, you discover an impending invasion that threatens humanity. It's up to you to use every state-of-the-art weapon at your disposal, whether you're battling in Twilight City or venturing into outer space. Your mission is to save humanity before it's too late. The story is a work in progress, so stay tuned for updates!

Project Repository

The source code and assets for Luke Ken can be found on GitHub: https://github.com/LukeKenTeam/LukeKen

Get Involved

Whether you're an artist, modder, or FOSS enthusiast, you can contribute to the project. Join our community, share your assets, collaborate, or contribute to the existing resources. Visit the GitHub repository for more information on how to get involved.
