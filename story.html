The Storys of Luke Ken

The Origin Of Luke Ken

In his younger youth, he was well-versed in pop culture. One day he played and watched the Freedom Scoops series, which inspired him to become a hero. As he grew older became a adult, technology advanced, and there was even a cure for aging. It was already a futuristic culture. He was walking down a street when he saw a flyer and decided to join the Galactic Task Force (G.T.F.), so he began training. Then this marked the start of his journey to become a space enforcer and his future adventures.

Story Plot:

The events unfold in the year 2649. You assume the role of Luke Kensington (also known as Luke Ken), a futuristic Space Enforcer Commander. Luke is a young and attractive straight male in his early 30s, seemingly ageless due future ground braking advancements, A born hero inspired by Freedom Scoops in his early youth from the 21st century, working within the Galactic Task Force (GTF) and trained to be the best in his class, This takes place after returning from very dangerous mission (Issue #2 story) on another planet following a big battle where Luke Ken defeated an evil alien leader called Garrus that sent a fake distress call to their planet to trap him but failed, which provoked different evil alien races to start an invasion on Earth.
